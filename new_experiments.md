Logistic Regression (GLM - Binomial)Since the raw recognition accuracy at the trial level is a binary outcome (correct vs. incorrect), a logistic regression is highly appropriate.Research Question: To what extent do subjective confidence, frame type (Before Boundary vs. Event-Middle), and video editing style predict the probability of correct frame recognition?The Model: * Dependent Variable: Resp.corr (Binary: 1 for correct, 0 for incorrect).Independent Variables: Condition (_NB vs. _AB) , Target Type (BB vs. EM) , and Confidence Rating (Conf_radio.response).Why it works: The mid-report notes that the "boundary advantage" seems tied to high-confidence recollection. A logistic model can test this statistically by looking at the interaction between condition and confidence when predicting accuracy.
